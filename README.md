# Sales-Dashboard
This project is a dynamic dashboard designed to analyze sales performance across different types of vehicles on 2005. It enables users to explore trends, compare categories, and gain insights into overall sales patterns.
## Skills used
- Data cleaning using Power Pivot
- Formulas (If function, Text/data function)
- Pivot table 
- Pivot charts
- Charts (pie chart and line chart)
- Illustrations

### Dashboard analysis

#### The vehicle's overall sales has been bullish for the first 5 months of 2005. The sales, unit sold, and customers has increased by around 30 percent vs prior year. Based on the trend, 2005 has outperformed 2004 by every month from January to May which tells us that this year's better than the last. In terms of Region, EMEA has been the best region in terms of sales but in terms of country, USA is still the top country worldwide. 

#### On the other hand, Classic and vintage cars has been our top product which means that our revenue is not about the amount we sell but the quality we give to our consumers.

#### I personally recommend that we should focus on this current bullish scenario especially when we go to november which is the greatest month of the company for the last 2 years (2003-2004) and I also recommend to include the more attention to our classic cars as it is our best product for the last 3 years.
